A little about me!

Currently I'm a senior at Iowa State University, constantly working on side projects and open source, and drinking a lot of coffee! At ISU, I am the Vice President of the Computer Science and Software Engineering club, where I help to organize company events and game nights. I am also actively involved in helping to improve the Software Engineering Program as part of the Student Advisory Council. In the past, I've helped organize events for 200+ people as part of the Engineering Events Committee.

I was born in Muscat, Oman and grew up in Mumbai, India. I went back to Muscat when I was 14 and completed my high school. I once again switched things and came to US to pursue my higher education. These experiences provided me with significant global exposure and played a pivotal role in shaping my personality. I find the field of computer science very fascinating as it inspires creativity. I strongly believe that by creating rich and easy to use software products, I can make a positive impact on the lives of millions across the world.
